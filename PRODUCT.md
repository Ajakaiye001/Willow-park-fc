# Willow Park FC — Club Website

## Product Purpose
The official web home of Willow Park Football Club, a grassroots club in Athlone,
Co. Westmeath, Ireland, founded in 1972. The site must make a 50-year-old local
club feel as proud and permanent as it is: fixtures, teams, club story, and a
clear path to joining or supporting.

## Register
brand

## Users
- Local families deciding where their kids will play
- Current players and members checking fixtures and results
- Ex-players and the Athlone diaspora feeling nostalgic
- Local sponsors sizing the club up

## Brand & Tone
Voice words: **weathered, loud, communal.**
Physical object: a matchday programme printed on cheap stock, a painted
clubhouse sign, a woven crest patch stitched onto a red shirt.
Terrace-poster confidence, Irish grassroots warmth. Never corporate,
never "sports-tech".

## Colors
Red and black carry the brand (kit colors). The crest adds a green willow
tree, used only as a small ritual accent. Paper off-white for breathing
sections.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages (gradient heroes, icon-card grids)
- "Sports analytics dashboard" dark-neon aesthetics
- Premier League corporate gloss. This is grassroots and proud of it.

## Strategic principles
- Heritage is the asset: Estd. 1972 appears like a stamp, everywhere it earns it.
- Fixtures read like a printed programme, not a data table.
- Every section should feel paintable on the side of a clubhouse.
